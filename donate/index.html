For over 10 years, the Southern New Hampshire Pinball Club has been a place where people come together to play, learn, compete, and share a love of pinball.

Right now, we're in the middle of an important transition. We've made the decision to move on from our current space and are actively working toward a new home for the club.

Why This Matters

This club has always been about more than machines. It's about community, friendships, and keeping pinball alive for the next generation.

As we work toward our next chapter, your support helps us stay organized, keep things moving, and continue building something special.

How Your Support Helps

  • Transition and relocation efforts
  • Club operations during this interim period
  • Events and community engagement

All contributions go directly toward supporting the club and its mission to provide a welcoming space for pinball enthusiasts of all skill levels.

We Appreciate Your Support

If you'd like to support the club during this transition, we truly appreciate it.
