Your gift keeps our nonprofit pinball community moving while we relocate, repair, and reopen.

For over 10 years, SNHPC has brought people together to play, learn repairs, run leagues, and keep pinball culture thriving in Southern New Hampshire. During our transition to a new space, donations provide flexible funding for the work that gets us reopened.

Relocation & setup

Moving logistics, storage, setup supplies, and early buildout needs for the next location.

Machine preservation

Maintenance materials and parts so machines stay healthy and ready for public play.

Community continuity

Communication, planning, and member events that keep the club connected between locations.

Suggested giving levels

You can donate any amount. If helpful, here are common gift sizes and what they typically support:

  • $25 helps cover consumable repair supplies.
  • $50 helps fund move-day materials and transport costs.
  • $100 helps support larger setup and reopening expenses.
  • Monthly gifts provide stable support for ongoing club operations.

Donor transparency

SNHPC is volunteer-run. Donations are directed to club operations and transition costs, and we keep spending focused on mission-critical needs. Questions about how funds are used are always welcome at support@snhpinballclub.com.

Tax treatment can vary by donor and current nonprofit filings. Please consult your tax advisor for guidance on deductibility.
