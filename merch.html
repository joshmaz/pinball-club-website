SNHPC merch

The online store is still in the shop, but club gear is alive and well.

T-Shirts

Club logo shirts in assorted sizes. Great for tournaments, open houses, and looking like you know where the tilt bob lives.

Drink Koozies

Keep your beverage cold and your cabinet side art dry. Fits most cans and keeps ring marks off the repair table.

Thoughts & Prayers

Emotionally supportive, physically lightweight, and always in stock. Suggested donation accepted.

  3. We will confirm availability, total, and meetup/pickup details.
