The Southern New Hampshire Pinball Club hosts a variety of events throughout the year. In fact, we've been hosting pinball events at our club for over a decade. Sure, we've been running a New England Pinball League location since 2016, but we've also hosted a variety of other events, including open houses, tournaments, and more.

Events will be returning again as soon as we move in to our new home. Check out the Past Events section to see what ten years of events looks like!
