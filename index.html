Welcome to the Southern New Hampshire Pinball Club!

We are Moving to a New Space! (We hope...)

For the past ten amazing years, we've been bringing pinball enthusiasts together at our current location. Now, we're turning the page to an exciting new chapter.

We've decided not to renew our lease at our beloved—if slightly infamous—“Murder Warehouse.” We've had death metal bands next door, bathrooms that earned us a tongue-in-cheek award, and countless memories.

But it's time for a fresh start.

We're actively searching for a new home in the Southern New Hampshire area—one that's brighter, more welcoming, and ready to host our growing community.

Here's where you come in:

For current members, your continued support is invaluable. Stick with us as we make this transition.

For past members, we'd love to see you again. Come check out what's next and consider renewing your membership.

If you've never heard of us, this is your invitation to join a passionate community of pinball lovers.

We're excited about what's ahead, but we can't do it alone. It's going to take memberships, donations, resources, and a whole lot of heart and elbow grease to make this happen. Together, we can build a new space that keeps the pinball spirit alive for years to come.

Help keep the Southern New Hampshire Pinball Club alive!

Stay tuned for updates, and thanks for being part of our journey.
