Welcome

We’re a member-driven club built on keeping games playable, sharing repair know-how, and welcoming anyone who loves silverball—from classic EM to the newest titles. Competitive players, casual flippers, and folks learning repairs are all at home here. Membership helps sustain the club through this transition; unlimited access to the space returns when we reopen in a new home. Read our story for how it works and where we came from.
