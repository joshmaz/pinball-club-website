Member sign-in

Hop in. The member portal is where we run the club online: games, events, machine issues, photos, and more as we keep building.

New here? Create an account (it’s free), confirm your email, then sign in below. Takes a minute.

Glad you’re here

If you play with us at SNHPC, or you’re thinking about it, this page is how you get into the member portal. New here? Enter your email and password below and click Sign Up. We will send a short confirmation message to that address; open the link in it to verify your email, then come back here and use Login with the same email and password. Already set up? Just log in. No secret handshake required, just don’t tilt the form.

Your account on this site

Creating and using an account on this website is free. That’s just your login and profile here. It is not the same thing as optional paid floor membership through the club’s billing when you join that path. We’ll keep those ideas clearly labeled as the portal grows.

Soon: we’re working toward letting you manage your on-site club membership from the member portal itself (think fewer hoops when it’s time to renew or update how you pay). Not live yet. We’ll shout when it ships.

What’s in the member portal

Behind this sign-in, the club runs a lot of day-to-day work: games catalog and floor history, events, machine issue reports (Pinball Map imports and club-wide notes), photo library tools, and membership admin for folks who help run the roster. Not everyone sees every panel. You’ll only get the sections that match the roles you’ve been assigned. If you volunteer, we can open more tools over time as it makes sense.

New chapter, new website

SNHPC is writing a fresh chapter at a new home, and this site is part of that story. It’ll get better looking; right now we’re chasing function first so the lineup, calendar, and issue list stay trustworthy while we wire things up. Thanks for bearing with a site that’s still a workshop, not a showroom.

How you can help

We’re a nonprofit, member-driven club, and volunteers keep the lights on. Whether you like wrenching games, tinkering with the web, or helping keep a steady cadence of on-site events for everyone, there’s room for you. Sign in, poke around the portal, and grab a board member or volunteer lead at league or open play if you want to pitch in.
