# CONTENT_GUIDE.md: SNH Pinball Club Website Content & Messaging Guide

---

# 🎯 Purpose

This guide defines the **voice, tone, and messaging strategy** for the Southern New Hampshire Pinball Club website.

It ensures:

- Consistent communication
- Strong community identity
- Clear and effective calls to action
- Alignment with nonprofit values

---

# 🧭 Core Messaging Principles

## 1. Community First

- Emphasize people over machines
- Highlight inclusivity and shared experiences

## 2. Transparency

- Be honest about goals, needs, and changes
- Especially important for donations and memberships

## 3. Low Pressure

- Invite participation
- Avoid guilt-based messaging

## 4. Fun + Nostalgia

- Pinball is playful and social
- Tone should reflect that

---

# 🗣️ Voice & Tone

## Overall Voice

- Friendly
- Welcoming
- Slightly playful
- Authentic (not corporate)

## Avoid

- Corporate jargon
- Overly technical language
- Aggressive sales language

---

# 💬 Examples

### ✅ Good

- “Come flip with us.”
- “We’re excited about what’s next.”
- “Join the community.”

### ❌ Avoid

- “Act now before it’s too late!”
- “Limited time offer!”
- “Exclusive premium access tiers”

---

# 💰 Donation Messaging

## Goals

- Explain why support matters
- Show impact
- Keep tone appreciative

## Structure

### 1. Context

Explain what’s happening:

- Moving locations
- Expanding
- Improving the club

### 2. Invitation

Invite support:

- “If you’d like to help...”
- “Consider contributing...”

### 3. Gratitude

Always thank:

- “We appreciate your support.”
- “Thanks for being part of this community.”

---

# 👥 Membership Messaging

## Focus Areas

- Belonging
- Access
- Community

## Key Points

- Membership = supporting the club
- Membership = access to events and space
- Membership is not a “product purchase”

---

# 📢 Call-to-Action (CTA) Guidelines

Keep CTAs:

- Short
- Clear
- Action-oriented

## Examples

- “Join the Club”
- “Become a Member”
- “Scan to Learn More”
- “Support the Move”

---

# 🖼️ Visual + Copy Alignment

- Images should feel real and community-driven
- Avoid overly stock-looking imagery
- Pair visuals with short, punchy text

---

# 🧩 Page-Specific Guidance

## Home Page

- Quick overview
- What the club is
- How to get involved

## Donate Page

- Story + purpose
- Clear CTA
- Easy to access via QR code

## Members Page

- Status clarity
- Simple UI
- No confusion

---

# 🔮 Future Content Ideas

- Member spotlights
- Event recaps
- Machine highlights
- “Behind the scenes” posts

---

# 🧠 AI Content Rules

When generating content:

- Keep sentences short and readable
- Prefer clarity over cleverness
- Match the club’s tone (friendly, community-driven)
- Avoid overhyping or exaggeration

---

# ❤️ Final Guiding Principle

If it doesn’t feel like something you’d say to someone standing next to a pinball machine…

…it doesn’t belong on the site.
