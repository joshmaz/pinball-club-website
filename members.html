How membership works today

Members pay monthly or annually, in cash or by recurring PayPal, and the treasurer updates your record after each payment.

  • Monthly: $40
  • Annual: $480
  • Pay by PayPal: paypal.me/snhpinball
  • Pay in cash: hand it to the treasurer at the club

What membership gets you

24/7 pass access to the club's pinball room on Bridge St in Nashua, with every game on free-play. Just stopping by for an event? Visitors can grab an all-day guest pass for $10 per visit.

Memberships help keep this nonprofit pinball club afloat. If you want to push the club ahead in bigger ways, a donation is the most direct boost.

Club & machine notes

A shared place to track machine issues, floor notes, and club tasks so other helpers know what needs attention. Some machine-condition notes arrive from Pinball Map submissions; others are filed here in the portal. Every signed-in member can read the list and add a note. Each note shows who or what submitted it when that is known. Members with a portal helper role (events, photos, games, or membership) can edit existing notes and move status between Incoming, In progress, and Resolved.
